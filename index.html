How to take a screenshot:

Full Screen: Press Command Shift 3 to capture the entire screen.

How to submit assignments on Canvas:

To submit an assignment on Canvas, navigate to your course, click "Assignments," and select the specific task. Click "Start Assignment" (top right), then choose your submission type (File Upload, Text Entry, Website URL, or Google Drive). Agree to the end-user license agreement, click "Submit Assignment," and verify the confirmation timestamp.

How to use common keyboard shortcut:

Copy: Press Command C to copy the selected item to the clipboard.

Paste: Press Command V to paste the contents of the clipboard into the current document or app.

Cut: Press Command X to cut the selected item and copy it to the clipboard.
